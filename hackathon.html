About REC HACKATHON

A hackathon is an event where individuals come together to engage in rapid and collaborative engineering over a short period, typically ranging from 24 to 48 hours.

The primary goal of a hackathon is to create functional software or hardware by the end of the event. Participants include programmers, graphic designers, product managers, and domain experts working intensively on innovative projects.

Hackathons may focus on themes such as technology stacks, domains (like AI or Blockchain), or specific social challenges.
