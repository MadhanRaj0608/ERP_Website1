About IEEE REC

The IEEE Club at REC is dedicated to fostering innovation and technical excellence in various fields of engineering and technology. We host workshops, bootcamps, and conferences to inspire and educate students.

IEEE Society members stay technically current, network with colleagues locally and abroad, and collaborate on research and projects with leading experts -- all while taking advantage of specialized opportunities. Discover which Society best matches your technical interests and gain access to cutting-edge technical journals, conference discounts, resources, and global networking.
